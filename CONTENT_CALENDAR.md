| Pillar | % of Content | Description |
|--------|--------------|-------------|

- **Target**: 2-4 posts per month
- **Languages**: Publish in both EN and JP (adapted, not direct translation)

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Outline** (Day 1)
   - Define key points
   - Research keywords
   - Plan structure

2. **Draft** (Day 2-3)
   - Write first draft
   - Apply brand voice
   - Run through quality checklist

3. **Edit** (Day 4)
   - Read aloud for flow
   - Check for AI-sounding phrases
   - Ensure varied sentence structure

4. **Translate/Adapt** (Day 5)
   - Adapt for Japanese audience
   - Adjust cultural references
   - Verify tone appropriateness

5. **Publish** (Day 6)
   - Add to both EN and JP sites
   - Update blog indexes
   - Share on social (if applicable)
